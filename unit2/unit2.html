Who Are They?

The Pillows are a Japanese alternative rock band that were founded in 1989, having released 22 albums and 40 singles. They were popularized worldwide by the FLCL OVA series, which licenced their songs for its soundtrack.

Who's In It?

The Pillows have 4 current members & 2 past members.

  • Current members:
    1. Sawao Yamanaka (Vocals, Guitar)
    2. Yoshiaki Manabe (Guitar)
    3. Shinichirou Sato (Drums)
    4. Jun Suzuki (Bass)
  • Past members:
    1. Kenji Ueda
    2. Tatsuya Kashima
